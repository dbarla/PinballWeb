Paragon Pinball Machine: A Classic Fantasy Adventure

Released in 1979 by Bally, the Paragon pinball machine stands as a testament to the golden age of pinball, bringing a blend of fantasy and challenging gameplay to enthusiasts worldwide. Its vivid artwork and intricate playfield design make it a standout title in the era of wide-body pinball machines, offering a unique experience that is both visually and mechanically engaging.

Design and Theme

The Paragon pinball machine immerses players in a fantastical world filled with mythical creatures and epic landscapes. The artwork, created by the renowned Paul Faris, depicts a grand, otherworldly scene replete with dragons, warriors, and mystical beasts. This rich visual backdrop is complemented by the wide-body format, which allows for a more expansive playfield and an array of unique features.

Playing the Game

Playing Paragon requires skill and strategy. Here are some key elements to master:

1. The Beast’s Lair

One of the most iconic features of Paragon is the "Beast’s Lair," a challenging area on the playfield. To navigate it successfully:

  • Skillful Nudging: The Beast’s Lair is notorious for trapping balls, making it essential to use skillful nudging to avoid getting stuck and to recover balls effectively.
  • Controlled Shots: Precision is key when shooting towards the Beast’s Lair. Aim to hit the designated targets without losing control of the ball.
  • Escape Strategies: If the ball becomes trapped, use careful nudging to guide it out of the lair and back into active play.

2. Strategic Gameplay Tips

To achieve high scores in Paragon, players should focus on the following strategies:

  • Maximise Bonus Multiplier: Increasing the bonus multiplier is crucial for scoring. Focus on hitting targets that increase the multiplier for greater end-of-ball bonuses.
  • Utilise the In-Line Drop Targets: The game features three in-line drop targets that are central to gameplay. Clearing these targets in sequence can unlock significant scoring opportunities.
  • Ball Control: Due to the wide-body design, ball control and precise shooting are essential. Focus on making controlled shots to the key areas of the playfield.

Notable Features

Paragon includes several unique and engaging features:

  • Wide-Body Design: The wider playfield offers more space for complex features and targets, enhancing the gameplay experience.
  • Fantasy Artwork: The intricate and detailed artwork sets a fantasy tone, immersing players in an epic adventure.
  • Challenging Playfield Layout: The layout includes various elements like the Beast’s Lair, in-line drop targets, and multi-level play areas that provide depth and challenge.

Final Thoughts

The Paragon pinball machine is a classic that continues to captivate players with its unique blend of challenging gameplay and stunning fantasy artwork. Its wide-body format and intricate design set it apart from other machines of its era, offering a rich and rewarding experience for pinball enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned player or a newcomer, Paragon promises an adventure that is both challenging and unforgettable.
